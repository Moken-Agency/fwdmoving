Rates

2023 Rates & Promotions

Moving Rates

  • Basic price for moving help is $125.00 per hour for two movers, with additional charges for extra help

  • The Fwd Moving Company offers hourly rates for two movers and a moving truck for $165.00 per hour

  • Additional movers can be hired for $55.00 per hour.

For example three movers and a van would cost $220.00 per hour.

Packing Rates

  • Packing services are also offered at a basic price of $125.00 per hour for two packers

  • Additional packers can be hired for $60.00/hour

  • The Fwd Moving Company provides a range of packing supplies, including cardboard boxes, plastic boxes, bubble wrap, and labels

Hourly Minimums

  • Minimum time requirements vary region by region. However, typically a standard minimum of four hours is charged, if the task is completed in less than five hours.

  • Free plastic boxes are only available for moves with a minimum time of five hours

  • Damage coverage options are available and will affect the moving price

Special Rates & Promotions

  • Special rates and promotions are offered for local and long-distance services

  • The Fwd Moving Company offers flexible scheduling and can accommodate last-minute moves

  • Moving supplies are available at an additional cost and are necessary for ensuring the protection and cleanliness of client belongings.

Quotes & Price Variances

  • Moving quotes and prices may vary based on: type of moving truck needed, distance to destination, use of free-of-charge totes, and distance from current location

  • Flat rate quotes can be arranged by calling the office and scheduling a time for a free estimate.

  • Extra fees may apply for premium and heavy items, stairs, long carries, and waiting time.

A deposit of $150 is required upon booking and payment is due upon completion of services. A 5% GST charge applies to all rates. We accept all types of credit cards, cash and e-transfer when available.
